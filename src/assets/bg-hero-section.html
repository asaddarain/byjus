About BYJU’S

BYJU’S is India’s largest education technology company and the creator of India’s most-loved learning app. Launched in 2015, BYJU’S offers highly personalized learning programs for grades k-12. With 50 million registered students and 3.5 million paid subscriptions, BYJU’S has become one of the most preferred education platforms across the globe.

Frequently asked questions

  • What is BYJU’S FutureSchool?

    BYJU’S FutureSchool is an innovative learning platform that offers online private tutoring and small group classes. Our research-based, hands-on, interactive curriculum inspires creative thinking and original projects. We offer students coding for grades 1-12, music for grades 1-12, and math for grades 1-8, with additional advanced math classes in development. Courses in science and fine arts are scheduled to launch soon.
    The BYJU’S FutureSchool platform is now available in seven countries: the United States, Mexico, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, Brazil, and Indonesia. As we design a school of the future, our mission is to help transform children into confident, active learners and creators by inspiring them to explore and create mobile apps, websites, games, music, and art.

  • What will my child learn in the free coding class?

    In the live, 1-on-1 free coding class with an expert teacher, your child will have the opportunity to learn sequences, loops, variables, and objects to create their own animations and apps. Upon successful completion of the class, your child will receive their personalized Coding Fundamentals Certificate.

  • What does my child need to have in order to take the free coding class?

    Your child is required to join the free coding class via a laptop or desktop computer with a working camera and microphone. A parent must also join the class for the first 10 minutes in order to give permission for their child to take the class. The teacher will also answer the parent’s questions and explain the curriculum and its real-world applications.

  • What devices support the free coding classes?

    Our classes can be taken by any laptop or desktop computer. Mobile devices are not supported currently.

  • Who will be teaching my child?

    Your child will begin their coding journey with an expert teacher. All our instructors go through a rigorous, five-step selection process to be certified, with a competitive acceptance rate of 1%. Our teachers use question-based techniques that let kids find their own solutions, but our teachers’ styles are as unique as they are! Together, our teachers have delivered more than 8 million hours of coding training. You can see live feedback from our students by visiting our Live Teacher Dashboard.

  • Why are Tynker and BYJU’S FutureSchool working together?

    Both Tynker and BYJU’S FutureSchool are part of the overall BYJU’S ecosystem. BYJU’S is the world’s largest education technology company and the creator of India’s most-loved learning app. Launched in 2015, BYJU’S offers highly personalised and effective learning programs for classes K-12. With 100 million registered students and 7 million paid subscriptions, BYJU’S has become one of the most preferred education platforms across the globe. By joining forces with Tynker, BYJU’S is bringing imagination to life for hundreds of millions more students, through coding.

  • Why does BYJU’S require a phone number?

    We request your phone number in order to communicate with you about setting up the free trial class. Your phone number also allows us to answer your questions, solve technical issues, and ensure the best customer care experience. In addition, your phone number is helpful in order for us to evaluate, develop, and improve our products and services, as well as to send you information about other products or services you may like (you can always opt out of such communications by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link).

  • What happens after the free class?

    Your child will receive their Coding Fundamentals Certificate upon completing their class. In addition, our academic counselor will reach out to you to discuss your child’s trial coding report card and answer any questions you might have. Our counselor can also guide you through the different opportunities our program offers.

  • How does BYJU’S FutureSchool make online learning safe for children?

    BYJU’S FutureSchool adheres to strict guidelines to protect the privacy and safety of our students. Please refer to our Privacy Policy on how we secure your data and keep your child safe during our 1-on-1 online learning sessions. In addition, BYJU’S FutureSchool takes compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) very seriously. We are proud to be COPPA-certified and to have earned the kidSAFE+ Seal. You can view our certification here.
