BYJU’S FutureSchool is an innovative learning platform that offers online private tutoring and small group classes. Our research-based, hands-on, interactive curriculum inspires creative thinking and original projects. We offer students coding for grades 1-12, music for grades 1-12, and math for grades 1-8, with additional advanced math classes in development. Courses in science and fine arts are scheduled to launch soon. The BYJU’S FutureSchool platform is now available in seven countries: the United States, Mexico, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, Brazil, and Indonesia. As we design a school of the future, our mission is to help transform children into confident, active learners and creators by inspiring them to explore and create mobile apps, websites, games, music, and art.

In the live, 1-on-1 free coding class with an expert teacher, your child will have the opportunity to learn sequences, loops, variables, and objects to create their own animations and apps. Upon successful completion of the class, your child will receive their personalized Coding Fundamentals Certificate.

Your child is required to join the free coding class via a laptop or desktop computer with a working camera and microphone. A parent must also join the class for the first 10 minutes in order to give permission for their child to take the class. The teacher will also answer the parent’s questions and explain the curriculum and its real-world applications.

Our classes can be taken by any laptop or desktop computer. Mobile devices are not supported currently.

Your child will begin their coding journey with an expert teacher. All our instructors go through a rigorous, five-step selection process to be certified, with a competitive acceptance rate of 1%. Our teachers use question-based techniques that let kids find their own solutions, but our teachers’ styles are as unique as they are! Together, our teachers have delivered more than 8 million hours of coding training. You can see live feedback from our students by visiting our Live Teacher Dashboard.
